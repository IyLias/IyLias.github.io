## Regular Graph

If $\delta(G) = \Delta(G)$, then the vertices of G have the same degree and $G$ is called $regular$

If $deg \; v = r$ for every vertex $v$ of $G$, where $0 \le r \le n-1$, then $G$ is $r-regular$ or 
$regular \;of \;degree \;r$
